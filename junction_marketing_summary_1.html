How to Qualify

Good tenants deserve to be treated like good tenants.

The Founding Member Package — including the last month's deposit waiver — is designed for applicants who come prepared. If your application is strong, we reward that from day one.

  • ✓
    Credit score of 700+ — a strong credit history demonstrates reliability and earns you the deposit waiver
  • ✓
    Verified income at 2.5–3× monthly rent — employment letter on company letterhead with salary confirmation
  • ✓
    Rental history or professional references — two previous landlord references or equivalent
  • ✓
    Government-issued photo ID — clear scan required with application
  • ✓
    Lease signed within first 90 days — Founding Member pricing and deposit waiver are time-limited
