How to Qualify

Good tenants deserve to be treated like good tenants.

The Founding Member Package — including the no last month's deposit — is designed for applicants who come prepared. Here's what we look for:

  • ✓
    Credit score of 700 or higher — a strong credit history qualifies you for the deposit waiver
  • ✓
    Income of 2.5 to 3 times the monthly rent — confirmed with an employment letter on company letterhead
  • ✓
    Rental history or professional references — two previous landlord references or equivalent
  • ✓
    Government-issued photo ID — a clear scan submitted with your application
  • ✓
    Lease signed within the first 90 days — Founding Member pricing and the deposit waiver are time-limited
